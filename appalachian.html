appalachian mountain advocates

social activism

The Appalachian Mountain Advocates are a group of attorneys that are fighting for the end of mountaintop removal mining practices. They were in need of a regional campaign to raise awareness and to reach out to the people affected by these practices. A new logo was also created for the project.

Posters, fliers, and coasters were created to distribute among the affected towns. I thought the coasters could be particularly effective as a discussion piece where people congregate at bars and cafes.
