Hi, my name is Drew Nelson. I'm a graphic designer living and loving in Lincoln, Nebraska. I'm originally from the great Pacific Northwest and have lived all over the country and experienced many great things and met lots of lovely people. I have walked in many shoes: (baker, chef, record store guy, barista, history student, vegetable farmer, interpretive hike leader, after-school programmer, cheese-maker, fermenter, and traveller.) To be quite honest, I loved every one of these experiences, and believe that they help me in my practice and love of graphic design! My goal is to create unique and positive design that will help make changes in the world and bring people together.

If you like what you see on this website, let me know! I can't wait to meet more people who dig design and want to share ideas or work together!
