Hi, my name is Drew Nelson. I am a graphic designer living and loving in Lincoln, Nebraska. I am originally from Gig Harbor, Washington but I have lived all over the country and have experienced many great things and met lots of lovely people. I have been many things: (baker, chef, record store guy, barista, history student, vegetable farmer, interpretive hike leader, after-school programmer, cheese-maker, fermenter, and traveller.) To be quite honest: I loved every one of these things, and I believe that my past experiences really help me in my practice and love of graphic design!

If you like what you see on this website, let me know! I can't wait to meet more people who love design and who want to share ideas or work together!
