Friends of Wilderness Park

Catalog

Wilderness Park is a city park in Lincoln, Nebraska that has a volunteer group which helps maintain trails and pay for improvements. They have an annual plant sale that helps generate funding for their endeavors as the Friends of Wilderness Park. They needed a new fully illustrated catalog.

The primary audience for this catalog are homeowners with a love of nature who wish to plant native Nebraska plants in their yards. I wanted to hand draw every plant and give the catalog an organic and handmade feel.
