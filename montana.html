spirit of montana

Brand Identity System

Spirit of Montana distilling is the oldest micro distillery in Montana. I wanted to redesign their branding to help attract a younger audience.

I created a system of shapes that could be used to make letters and chose cool colors that would a feel fresh, clean and crisp. These attributes should go hand in hand with the spirits they create and also with Montana.
