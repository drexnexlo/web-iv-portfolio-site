audubon society

ad campaign

The Audubon Society is a grassroots non-profit organization devoted to the preservation and maintenence of wild bird habitat. They needed a national ad campaign.

Included in the campaign were three print ads, environmental signage found at parks, and an on-line pop-up donation window.
