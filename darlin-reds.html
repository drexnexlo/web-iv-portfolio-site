The Darlin' Reds

Identity

The Darlin' Reds is a small vegetable farm located outside Lincoln, Nebraska. It's ran by two young women who love growing vegetables and providing food to their CSA subscribers. They were in need of a logomark and identity system.

The Darlin' Reds are very positive and friendly - with a touch of attitude. I wanted to provide them with an approachable and warm identity system that was at once friendly and memorable.
