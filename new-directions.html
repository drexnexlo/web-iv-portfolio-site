new directions catalog

type-heavy publication

New Directions is a publishing house in New York City who publish transalted works, classics, and new fiction from intellectual literary authors. I took exsisting content from their 2016 Winter and Spring catalog and created a new layout.

The cover is reflective of my interest in Swiss typography, and I had been wanting to play around with some of those elements. The interior was really more of an exercize in using lots of body copy in a small space. I really enjoy these kinds of problems and had fun making this catalog.
