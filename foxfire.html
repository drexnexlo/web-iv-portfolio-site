foxfire

Book Design

Foxfire is a series of books produced in the 1960's by high school students and their instructor. Their mission was to document and record many of the folk arts, tales, traditions, and techniques used by the older people in their region of Appalachia. The books were a perfect candidate for a redesign.

I really wanted to keep with a traditional feeling, but also make the books more contemporary and fun to flip through.
