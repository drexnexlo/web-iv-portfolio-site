wfmu

Ad Campaign

WFMU is a listener supported radio station in Jersey City, New Jersey. I love their programming and decided to create a fundraising campaign for them.

I thought about a little guy riding a bike up a mountain - getting higher and higher as donations came in. There are 3 print ads that would run in the Village Voice, an animation for their homepage, and a wooden sign which would be a 'fundraise -o- meter'.
