rollo art pens

package design

Rollo art pens needed a complete package design and rebranding. I took their exsisting brand and completely scrapped it for a more colorful and playful feeling.

Rollo liked the concept of having a set build a picture when the boxes are arranged a certain way. Great care was taken to ensure that the pens looked great side by side no matter which sides were facing the viewer.
